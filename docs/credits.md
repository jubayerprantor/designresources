# Credits & Design

Behind this beautiful, high-performance Design Resource Hub is a collaboration of creative design tools and agentic AI technology.

---

## 🎨 Creative & Visual Design: Canva

The beautiful, glassmorphic 3D composition floating on our homepage was designed in **Canva**. 

Canva's modern, accessible creative suite enabled the layout, aesthetic palettes, and smooth visual feedback that form the artistic backbone of this hub. The drifting glow particles, glossy translucent materials, and rotating gold toruses were all crafted to represent the intersection of design and technology.

---

## 🤖 Engineering & Integration: Antigravity (by Google DeepMind)

The infrastructure, robust data-parsing, category optimization, and responsive design integrations were developed by **Antigravity**, a powerful agentic AI coding assistant designed by the **Google DeepMind** team.

Antigravity worked directly with the project codebase to:
- Overhaul the data ingestion pipelines to parse **3,100+ raw spreadsheet records** cleanly.
- Maintain high-quality curation by deduplicating entries across multiple priority tiers.
- Embed Canva's interactive CSS keyframes natively as a Vue component in VitePress.
- Build custom layout fixes to keep the category grids perfectly centered and mobile-responsive.
